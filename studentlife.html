Life at Indiana University

Clubs, housing, dining, athletics, and traditions that shape the IU experience.

Campus Facilities & Housing

Indiana University offers modern residence halls, apartment-style housing, libraries, and recreation centers. Students live and learn in a supportive environment with quick access to dining, fitness, and study spaces.

Dining on Campus

Dining Halls

Multiple all-you-can-eat dining halls with diverse cuisines, including vegetarian and vegan options.

Cafés & Coffee

Grab a latte or quick snack between classes at campus cafés and coffee shops.

Local Eats

Enjoy Bloomington’s vibrant food scene with international restaurants just steps from campus.

Clubs & Organizations

Academic Clubs

Professional groups to connect with peers, mentors, and career opportunities.

Sports & Recreation

Over 25 varsity teams, intramural leagues, and state-of-the-art gyms for all fitness levels.

Social & Cultural Clubs

From music ensembles to cultural groups, there’s a community for every interest.

IU Traditions & Events

IU is home to long-standing traditions like the Little 500 bike race, homecoming festivities, and cultural festivals. Students experience a vibrant campus life full of music, sports, art, and community events year-round.

Student Support Services

Health & Wellness

Counseling, health clinics, and wellness programs for student well-being.

Career Services

Internship connections, résumé support, and career fairs prepare students for success.

Advising

Academic advisors help guide students through majors, courses, and graduation planning.
