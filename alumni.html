Notable Alumni

Mark Cuban

Entrepreneur | Investor | NBA Owner

Mark Cuban, a graduate of the Kelley School of Business, built his career through bold investments and innovation in technology. Today, he is best known as the outspoken owner of the Dallas Mavericks and a prominent figure on Shark Tank.

Jane Pauley

Journalist | Television Host

Jane Pauley earned her degree in Political Science at IU and went on to become one of America’s most trusted journalists. She now anchors CBS Sunday Morning, bringing thoughtful reporting to millions of viewers.

Suzanne Collins

Author | Storyteller

Best known for writing The Hunger Games trilogy, Suzanne Collins studied drama and telecommunications at IU. Her work has become a global phenomenon, inspiring blockbuster films and sparking conversations about power, society, and resilience.
