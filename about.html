About Indiana University

Indiana University is a world-class public research institution with a proud tradition of academic excellence, innovation, and community impact. Founded in 1820, IU has grown into one of the nation’s leading universities, offering more than 930 academic programs across multiple campuses. With a vibrant community of students, faculty, and alumni, Indiana University is dedicated to shaping leaders, advancing knowledge, and making a difference locally and globally. At IU, learning extends beyond the classroom. From groundbreaking research and top-ranked programs to spirited traditions and a thriving campus life, our students gain the tools and experiences needed to succeed in a diverse and ever-changing world. Guided by our core values of discovery, inclusion, and service, Indiana University continues to prepare the next generation of innovators, problem solvers, and global citizens.
